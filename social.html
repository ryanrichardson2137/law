What to know about SSDI

It can take up to six months to get disability, so don’t wait long to contact an attorney. If you’re new to the disability application process, here are a few more things you should know:

  • You must prove that your physical or mental health will keep you from holding a regular job for at least a year.
  • You should apply as soon as you become disabled to limit the time between your last paycheck and first disability check.
  • The state agency will speak with your doctors and look into your current medical files. If more information is needed, you will be contacted.
